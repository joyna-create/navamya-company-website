Product / Service Information

Introduction

An economic, long-lasting and high-quality solution for the chemical process industry. Designed using paste extrusion technology and built to meet international specifications (ASTM / DIN / BS / ANSI / JIS), this product ensures total quality from raw materials to final output. Comprehensive support is provided — from design to after-sales — through an expert team of engineers and technicians.

Product Range

  • Pipes up to 150mm Nominal Bore
  • Fittings up to 150mm Nominal Bore
  • Bellows up to 200mm Nominal Bore
  • Ball Valves up to 150mm Nominal Bore
  • Check Valves up to 100mm Nominal Bore
  • Strainers up to 100mm Nominal Bore
  • Sight Glass (PTFE / FEP lined) up to 100 NB
  • Dip Tubes, PTFE Lined Hose Assemblies
  • Manhole Covers (SS/MS)
  • PTFE Coated Receivers & Tanks
  • Lab PTFE Products & Mechanical Seal Pump Bellows

Quality Assurance

Every product goes through rigorous quality checks, including visual inspection, dimensional checks, tensile and specific gravity tests. Quality systems, documentation, and operating practices are strictly followed. Testing includes Spark Test (ASTM F1545), Hydro Test, and Type Test.

  • Surface Prep: Wire Brushing / Sand Blasting
  • Paint Options: Epoxy Primer / Chlorinated Rubber Paint / Enamel
  • Packing: PTFE Flare Faces with secure packaging
  • Third-party inspection & raw material test certificates available

Technical Data

  • Operating Pressure: Up to 20 bar / 300 psig (higher on request)
  • Vacuum Service: Heavy duty liner available (consult before use)
  • Temperature Range:
    • PTFE / PFA: Up to 230°C
    • FEP: Up to 180°C
    • ETFE: Up to 150°C
    • PVDF: Up to 130°C
  • Specifications:
    • Pipe: ASTM 106 Gr.B, ANSI B36.10, Schedule 40
    • Flange: ASTM A105/A181, ANSI B16.5 ASA 150
    • Fittings: ASTM A234 GRWPB, ANSI B16.9
    • Linings: As per ASTM F43
    • Minimum Flare Diameter: As per ASTM F432

Services Offered

  • Repair & Servicing of Glass-Lined Reactors (GLR)
  • SS Cladding & Jacket Replacement
  • Production of Anti-static Lined Pipes
  • Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)
  • Erection and Installation of GLRs

Fittings & Valves

  • Valves: Ball Valves, Butterfly Valves, Plug Valves, Non-return, Diaphragm
